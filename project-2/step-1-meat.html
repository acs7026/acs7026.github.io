Making Cheesesteaks

STEP 1: MEAT

Directions:

In a skillet, add in the shaved steak and diced onions, and cook on high until meat is brown.

As the meat is cooking, season it liberally with salt and pepper, stirring the meat and breaking it up as you stir.

When the meat is done cooking, drain it, and turn off the burner before returning the skillet to the stove top.

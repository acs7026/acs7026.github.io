STEP 2: MUSHROOMS

Directions:

Thoroughly wash one pound of sliced fresh Baby Bella mushrooms.

After mushrooms are washed, put them on a plate.

Melt 1/2 tablespoon of butter in a frying pan, then add all your mushrooms to the pan.

Sauté the mushrooms until dark brown and soft, with much of the mushrooms’ moisture having either evaporated or collected in the bottom of the pan with the melted butter.

Remove mushrooms from heat and return them to the plate they were previously on.

STEP 4: ROLLS & CHEESE

Directions:

Slice open the rolls (if not already open). Add in two slices of Provolone cheese. If desired, place roll with cheese into the toaster oven and toast until the cheese just starts to melt.

After the meat, mushrooms, and peppers are cooked, open the roll and place them inside in any order you see fit after adding the cheese. Add ketchup if desired.

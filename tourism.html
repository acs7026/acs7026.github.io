WELCOME TO CENTRAL PENNSYLVANIA!
Experience the Heart of the Keystone State! As the second state to join the Union, Pennsylvania offers so much to visitors! From historical battlefields to thriving amusement parks, Central Pennsylvania has something for almost everyone.
1
HERSHEYPARK:

Founded in 1906, this world-famous theme park draws in visitors from all over the planet! Ride legendary roller coasters like the Comet, Great Bear, and Sooperdooperlooper! Take a tour of Chocoloate World to see how Hershey's world-famous chocolate is made! Or stop in at ZooAmerica to see wildlife from all over our nation!

2
STATE CAPITOL:

The Pennsylvania State Capitol Building in Harrisburg. See the beautiful artwork that went in to making our capitol building one of the most ornate in the country! You'll also learn about our state's history, and see the halls of power in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.

See More
3
GETTYSBURG BATTLEFIELD:

The site of the turning point of the American Civil War. Fought in July of 1863, the Battle of Gettysburg saw the Union crush the Confederacy, marking one of the most critical moments in not just US, but world history! The battlefield is also home to many ghost tours, and has been called one of the most haunted places on Earth!

See More
4
PINE CREEK GORGE:

The Grand Canyon of Pennsylvania! See the gorgeous natural beauty of Pine Creek and its surrounding woodlands. A perfect place for hiking, fishing, camping, and all sorts of other outdoor fun!

See More
5
RENAISSANCE FAIRE:

Take a trip to the past at the Pennsylvania Renaissance Faire! See the world as it was during the Elizabethan Era, and experience the sights and sounds of a world of fantasy! Jousting, glassblowing, and human chess are just some of the events you'll experience here!
